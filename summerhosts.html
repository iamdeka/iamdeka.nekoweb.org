Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: What is this?
A: This is a list of hosting services that you should NOT use and should be wary of. They have very bad security, privacy and service standards, if any.
Summerhosts live on carding, abusing cloud providers and other illegal activities.

Q: Is this a full list?
A: No. This is not a complete list.
Research started 12/06/24, and due to the rapidly changing nature of summerhosts, this can become outdated in a few weeks, if not less.
If you've got anything to add, you should contact me.

Q: How do i contact you?
A: You can see my contacts on the index page.
If i change my accounts/contacts (highly unlikely), my page will be up to date.

Q: How can i tell if a host is a summerhost?
A: The most common symptoms of a summerhost:
No Terms of Service, no Privacy Policy, no public contact information, no public VAT registration number (not applicable under certain limits in Europe, these limits vary from country to country), review rewards, oversold (giving more resources than available hardware) servers, lack of basic features like databases and backups, improper billing (significantly better specs for boosting a Discord server, payment on Discord, et cetera).
Setup.md has a more extensive criteria for summerhosts, that you can check out here.

Q: How do i contribute?
A: You can provide information to me either in private (via Matrix, E-mail or Discord) or publicly (via Github in an issue or pull request).
You can also go to website archives (web.archive.org, archive.is) and make a copy of the websites that i link to (maybe even this website).
